Simple Sponge Cake

Ingredients

  • 3 egges
  • 100g castor suger
  • 85g self-raising flour

Note on Ingredients

Castor Suger
Finely granulated white suger
Self-raising flour
A pre-mixed combination of flour and leavening agents(usually salt and baking powder).

Method

  1. preheat the oven to 190C.
  2. grease a 20cm round cake pan.
  3. In a medium blow,whip together the egges ans castor sugar until fluffy.
  4. Fold in flour.
  5. Pour mixture into the prepared pan.
  6. Bake for 20 minutes in the preheated oven,or until the top cake springs back when light pressed.
  7. Cool in the pan over a wire rack.
